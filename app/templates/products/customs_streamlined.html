Common Questions

What are the GST requirements for imports?

All imports over NZ$1000 require GST payment at 15%. For goods under NZ$1000, overseas suppliers may collect GST at point of sale.

How does the TSW system work?

Trade Single Window is NZ's integrated border management system for lodging customs and biosecurity documentation electronically.

What are the main biosecurity requirements?

All food, plant, and animal products require MPI clearance. Some items may need permits or treatment before clearance.
